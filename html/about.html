About this app

A central requirement of the General Paper syllabus is that students need to stay up to date with the current affairs of the day. However, there are many news outlets publishing numerous articles on a wide range of topics on a daily basis. This can leave many students at a loss as to where to start when they resolve to cultivate a reading habit.

The NJC GP News Feed is the NJC English Department's response to this challenge, by providing our students with a resource to guide them along their reading journey.

Features:

 Daily curation of articles pertinent to the course
 Tagging of articles by topic
 Tagging of articles by past-year essay questions
 Search articles by keyword, topic, or past-year question
 Simple boolean search (AND, OR, NOT)
